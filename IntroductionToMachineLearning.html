Some of the hottest words in today's technical world are Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Neural Networks... They deserve to be! Artificial Intelligence has started touching our lives in every way and it will soon change almost every aspect of our life. This includes simple search predictions on Google and behavior prediction on Facebook, some wonderful products like Alexa or Siri, and also some miracles like driver-less cars and doctor-less surgeries that we all see on the horizon.
