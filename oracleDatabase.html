Oracle Database

The Oracle database is rightly named so. You need to be an oracle to understand it and guess what is hidden inside it; or what it is doing in the background; or to make it do what you want it to do.

Well not so bad, they have provided us great tools that can help us monitor and motivate it to do good work. Being a mature application, these problems as well as their solutions are now well known and well documented. But it takes effort to learn and understand each of them accurately. These blogs contain details of what I learnt while playing with the database.
